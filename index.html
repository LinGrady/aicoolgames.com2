Independent tech buying notes

Choose useful tech without getting pulled into the hype cycle.

Practical Tech Guide is maintained by Alex Morgan for readers comparing laptops, phones, AI tools, privacy software, home Wi-Fi, and everyday work gear. The focus is on trade-offs, setup realities, and when not to buy.

Decision-first guides

No fake lab claims
