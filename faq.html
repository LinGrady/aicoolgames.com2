Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about how the site works, how recommendations are written, and how to report issues.

What is Practical Tech Guide?

It is a small editorial site focused on practical buying advice, product comparisons, and technology explainers for readers who want clear decisions rather than hype.

How are recommendations chosen?

We look at usefulness, trade-offs, reader needs, and available product information. Paid placement does not control rankings.

How often are guides updated?

Guides are updated when a product, feature, price, or availability change makes the page less accurate or less useful.

Can readers suggest corrections?

Yes. Send correction notes to corrections@aicoolgames.com with the page title and the detail that needs review.

Is this professional advice?

No. The site provides general information and practical editorial guidance. Readers should verify current details with official providers before buying or relying on a product.
