Alex Morgan

Editor of Practical Tech Guide.

Editorial focus

Alex writes and maintains Practical Tech Guide with a focus on everyday buying decisions: what to upgrade, what to keep, what to test first, and what questions to ask before trusting a tool with work or personal data.

How recommendations are framed

The site does not claim a large testing lab or a hidden team of reviewers. When an article is a practical checklist rather than a formal benchmark, it is written that way. The goal is useful judgment, not fake certainty.
