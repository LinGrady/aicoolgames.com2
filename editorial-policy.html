Editorial Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

How we choose topics

We publish guides when a reader is likely to need a practical comparison, a buying decision, or a clearer explanation of a product category. We prefer topics with direct usefulness over broad trend coverage.

How we review products and tools

We look at what the product is for, who it suits, what it does well, where it falls short, and what current alternatives exist. When possible, guides are updated as products, pricing, or features change.

Editorial independence

We do not sell rankings. If a page contains affiliate links or other commercial relationships, those links do not decide the order of recommendations. Editorial judgment stays separate from advertising concerns.

Corrections and updates

If something is outdated or incorrect, readers can contact corrections@aicoolgames.com. We review reports and update pages when the change improves accuracy or clarity.

Use of AI assistance

We may use AI tools for drafting support, note-taking, or research assistance, but final content is reviewed and edited by a human before publication. We do not publish AI-generated filler or fake reviews.

Reader expectations

We aim for plain language, practical trade-offs, and useful next steps. The site is intended to help readers decide, not to overwhelm them with hype or oversized claims.

About Practical Tech Guide

Practical Tech Guide publishes clear, independent advice for choosing products, comparing tools, and understanding everyday technology.
