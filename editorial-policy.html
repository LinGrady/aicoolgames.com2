Editorial Policy & Content Guidelines

Last Updated: January 2026

Our Mission

TechPulse is committed to providing accurate, independent, and insightful technology journalism. Our mission is to help readers navigate the rapidly evolving technology landscape through in-depth analysis, expert reviews, and trustworthy reporting.

Editorial Independence

Separation of Advertising and Editorial

TechPulse maintains a strict separation between our advertising relationships and editorial content. Our editorial team operates independently from our business operations, and advertisers have no influence over our coverage.

Review Products

When reviewing products:

  • We purchase products whenever possible or clearly disclose loaned review units
  • Manufacturers have no preview or approval rights over reviews
  • We maintain objective scoring criteria across all product categories
  • Conflicts of interest are disclosed to readers

Affiliate Relationships

TechPulse participates in affiliate marketing programs. When readers purchase products through our affiliate links, we may earn a commission. This does not affect our editorial recommendations, which are based solely on product merit and reader value.

Content Standards

Accuracy

We are committed to accuracy in all our content:

  • Facts are verified through multiple sources when possible
  • Claims are supported by evidence and data
  • Corrections are prominently displayed when errors occur
  • Speculation is clearly labeled as such

Attribution and Sourcing

We properly attribute information and respect intellectual property:

  • Direct quotes are clearly attributed
  • Links are provided to original sources
  • Press releases are clearly identified when used as sources
  • We do not plagiarize or reproduce others' work without permission

Originality

All TechPulse content is original. We do not republish content from other sources without transformation, analysis, or additional reporting. Our goal is to add value through unique insights and expert perspective.

Review Methodology

Product Testing

Our product reviews follow rigorous testing protocols:

  • Standardized testing procedures ensure consistency
  • Real-world usage scenarios complement benchmark tests
  • Multiple team members evaluate products when appropriate
  • Testing periods are sufficient to identify long-term issues

Rating Criteria

Products are evaluated based on:

  • Performance: How well does the product perform its intended function?
  • Design: Is the product well-designed and built?
  • Usability: Is the product easy to use?
  • Value: Does the product offer good value for money?
  • Innovation: Does the product bring something new to the market?

Comparison Reviews

When comparing products:

  • Products are tested under identical conditions
  • Price points and target audiences are considered
  • Strengths and weaknesses of each product are fairly presented
  • Clear recommendations are provided based on user needs

Author Guidelines

Expertise

TechPulse authors are selected based on their expertise:

  • Relevant education or professional experience in their beat
  • Demonstrated knowledge of technology trends and developments
  • Strong writing and communication skills
  • Commitment to journalistic ethics

Conflicts of Interest

Authors must disclose potential conflicts:

  • Financial interests in companies they cover
  • Personal relationships with industry figures
  • Side projects or consulting work related to their beat
  • Any other situation that could compromise objectivity

Author Bios

All articles include author biographies that provide:

  • Relevant credentials and experience
  • Areas of expertise
  • Contact information or social media links

Corrections and Updates

Correction Policy

When we make mistakes, we correct them transparently:

  • Corrections are clearly labeled and dated
  • Significant corrections include an explanation
  • Original text is preserved when appropriate for transparency
  • Major corrections are noted at the top of articles

Updates

Articles are updated when:

  • New information becomes available
  • Products receive significant software updates
  • Prices change substantially
  • Earlier reporting is superseded by new developments

Updates are dated and summarized at the bottom of articles.

Reader Engagement

Comments Policy

We welcome reader comments that:

  • Contribute constructively to discussion
  • Are respectful of other readers
  • Do not contain hate speech, harassment, or threats
  • Are not spam or self-promotion

Comments violating these guidelines may be removed.

Feedback

Reader feedback is valued. We encourage readers to:

  • Report errors or inaccuracies
  • Suggest topics for coverage
  • Share their perspectives in comments
  • Contact our editorial team with concerns

AI and Automation Disclosure

TechPulse uses AI tools responsibly:

  • AI may be used for transcription, data analysis, or initial research
  • All published content is reviewed and verified by human editors
  • AI-generated content is clearly labeled when used
  • We do not use AI to generate fake reviews or testimonials

Diversity and Inclusion

TechPulse is committed to diversity in our coverage:

  • We seek diverse perspectives in our author team
  • Our coverage includes companies and products from various markets
  • We consider how technology impacts different communities
  • We actively work to reduce bias in our reporting
