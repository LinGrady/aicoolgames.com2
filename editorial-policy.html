Editorial Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Topic selection

Practical Tech Guide prioritizes topics where readers are likely to spend money, change a workflow, or share personal or work data with a tool.

Research and writing

Guides combine publicly available product details, practical setup considerations, privacy and support terms, and common reader trade-offs. The site avoids fabricated lab claims, fake awards, and exaggerated "best ever" rankings.

Ads and editorial independence

Advertising may appear on the site. Ads do not determine the wording of guides or the order of recommendations. Any paid placement or affiliate relationship that affects a page must be disclosed on that page.

Corrections and updates

Readers can send corrections to corrections@aicoolgames.com or through the contact page. Useful corrections are reviewed and incorporated when they improve accuracy or clarity.

AI assistance

AI tools may be used for drafting support, editing checks, or organization. Final editorial choices, claims, and publication decisions are reviewed by the site editor.
